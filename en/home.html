Portfolio

Boris Conrad Heißerer

Since 1994, my father bought a 486, computer games are an important part of my life. One of my first PC games was "Rebel Assault" I've played literally until the joystick was broken.

Through my »Interactive media systems studies« I was fully aware of the range of opportunities that can flow in a computer game. To figure out new game ideas and bringing together the different media has led me clearly to »Games Design«. These almost limitless possibilities put my own creativity to the test and reflects my personal motto perfectly
»Experience something new every single day« perfectly.

What distinguishes me is a desire to tear down the boundaries of regular thinking. My strengths are high enthusiasm for good ideas and the ability to be able to conclude these ideas alone or as part of a team.

I look forward to making new contacts and ideas from the games industry.
